Intelligent companion machine


虚拟朋友 音乐疗法 智能交互

项目设计说明
Design description

      这是一款基于自闭症孩子的一些核心症状设定和家庭干预作用的辅助智能产品，有研究表明音乐有助于改善自闭症儿童沟通能力等多种好处，在音乐中激励孩子主动表达。这款关于音乐的智能产品，有三种模式，每个模式都有益处，歌曲模式中，父母可以帮助孩子跟随音乐哼歌，做一些简单的动作(例如拍手，跺脚)，来增强孩子的语言沟通能力，和肢体语言的使用；乐器模式中，家长带领孩子跟随音乐有节奏通过击打虚拟乐器，来增强孩子的感知能力；在音乐游戏中，选择孩子感兴趣的，引起孩子学习注意，取代不良的顽固学习。希望通过这个项目设计能够在康复训练中有许些帮助。

     This is an auxiliary intelligent product based on some core symptom Settings of autistic children and the role of family intervention. Studies have shown that music can help improve the communication ability of autistic children and other benefits, and motivate children to express themselves actively in music. This smart product about music has three modes, each of which is beneficial. In the song mode, parents can help their children hum along to the music and do some simple movements (such as clapping and stomping) to enhance their children's verbal communication skills and body language use. In musical instrument mode, parents lead their children to follow the music and beat the virtual musical instrument to enhance their children's perception ability. In the music game, choose what the child is interested in to attract the child's learning attention, instead of bad stubborn learning. It is hoped that through the design of this project, there will be some help in rehabilitation training.

故事版/Version of the story

operation procedure.

家长可以根据孩子的年龄和病情阶段，选取适合的音乐和音乐游戏等，设置每个模式的使用时间。
Parents can select appropriate music and music games according to the child's age and stage of illness, and set the use time of each mode. 

孩子可以通过触摸拍打唤醒机器人，唤醒之后机器人会自动播放音乐。
he child or parents can wake up the small robot by touching and tapping, and the robot will automatically play calming music.

触摸交互，拍两下触角，切换到歌曲模式，同时播放视频。
Touch each other, tap your antennae twice, switch to song mode, and play the video. 

歌曲模式中,家长可以帮助孩子跟随音乐哼歌，做简单动作手势，增强身体语言的使用。
A video is played in the song mode, and parents can help their children hum along to the music or make simple gestures (for example, clapping, stomping, etc.) to enhance body language. 

孩子可以通过触摸交互切换乐器模式，选择自己喜欢的乐器，通过机器人慢慢的有节奏击打虚拟乐器，帮助孩子感觉发展作用，刺激感官发展。
Children can switch the musical instrument mode through touch interaction, choose their favorite musical instrument, and hit the virtual musical instrument slowly and rhythmically through the robot to help children feel the role of development and stimulate sensory development 

当孩子不使用时，家长和孩子可以按开关键关机。
Parents can press the switch to turn off the device when the child is not using it
